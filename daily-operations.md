# Claw Academy - Daily Operations

## Morning Routine (10 min)

1. **Check Dashboard**
   - Gumroad: Any new sales?
   - Email: Any questions?
   - Discord: New members?

2. **Social Check**
   - Reply to any comments
   - Engage with 3 posts
   - Check mentions

---

## Weekly Tasks

### Monday: Content
- [ ] Write 1 blog post
- [ ] Schedule social posts
- [ ] Email newsletter

### Tuesday: Engagement
- [ ] Reply to all comments
- [ ] Help in Discord
- [ ] Comment on Reddit

### Wednesday: Growth
- [ ] Outreach to 1 potential partner
- [ ] Apply to be guest on 1 podcast
- [ ] Share in 1 new community

### Thursday: Content
- [ ] Work on next lesson
- [ ] Record video (if ready)
- [ ] Update FAQ

### Friday: Review
- [ ] Check metrics
- [ ] Plan next week
- [ ] Celebrate wins!

### Weekend: Relax
- [ ] Optional: Weekly Zoom call

---
