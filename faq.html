🦶 Frequently Asked Questions

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant framework that runs locally on your computer. Unlike cloud-based AI services, your data stays on your machine and there are no monthly fees.

Do I need coding experience?

No! Our course starts from zero. If you can use a terminal/command line, you're good to go. We provide step-by-step instructions for everything.

What computer do I need?

Mac, Linux, or Windows (with WSL). At least 4GB RAM (8GB recommended). A Raspberry Pi can work for basic setups!

How much does it cost?

OpenClaw itself is free. The course is $67 one-time. Optional: $197/month for Mastermind with live calls.

Is this like ChatGPT?

Similar in that it's an AI, but you run it yourself. No data leaves your computer. You can customize it entirely. Plus you can connect it to your apps, smart home, and more.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! 30-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked.

How long does it take?

Basic setup: 10-30 minutes. Full course: 2-3 hours. After that, you can build and automate to your heart's content!

What's the time zone?

Classes are recorded, so you can take them anytime. Mastermind calls are typically on weekends (US Eastern time).
