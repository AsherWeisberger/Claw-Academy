## What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant framework that you can run locally on your own machine. Unlike ChatGPT or Claude where your data goes to the cloud, OpenClaw stays on your computer.

Think of it like having your own personal AI butler that:
- Lives on your hardware
- Connects to your apps (Telegram, Discord, email, etc.)
- Can control your smart home
- Runs 24/7 without subscription fees

---

## Why Run Your Own AI?

**Privacy First**
Your conversations, your data. Nothing leaves your machine unless you explicitly allow it.

**Customization**
Build exactly what you need. Not what the AI company thinks you need.

**No Monthly Fees**
Once it's set up, it's free to run. No per-message costs.

**Always Available**
Your own instance, always running, always ready.
