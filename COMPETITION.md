## Notes from Research

### What Works
- Personal stories ("I built X")
- Before/after transformations
- Clear outcomes ("In 2 hours you'll have...")
- Community/social proof
- Limited-time offers

### What Doesn't Work
- Too technical jargon
- Unrealistic promises
- No social proof
- Complicated pricing
